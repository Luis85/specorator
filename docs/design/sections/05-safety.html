section 05
Safety nets

Users need to feel safe experimenting. Three forgiveness paths — undo (24h), revert (full history), and spatial recovery ("where am I?"). Feature completion and archival live here too.

5a
Recovery — undo, revert, "where am I?"
Three distinct situations, one shared principle: the user should never feel trapped. Undo covers 24 hours. Revert covers the full feature history. "I'm lost" covers spatial disorientation.
