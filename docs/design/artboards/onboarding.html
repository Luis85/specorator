Welcome to Specorator
First time here?

Specorator helps you go from idea to shipped, one step at a time.

No methodology jargon required. The plugin walks you through 11 small steps, and tells you what to do at each one. Markdown stays the source of truth. AI is optional — off by default.

1. Plan it

Capture the idea, list the rules, sketch how it works.

2. Build it

Break into to-dos, write the code, prove the rules hold.

3. Ship it

Review, release, and look back so the next one's smoother.

You'll never be lost.
Every step has a "what is this?" link, an unobtrusive coach that tells you what to do next, and a way to undo if you change your mind.
